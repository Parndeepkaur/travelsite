Explore MONTREAL holidays and discover the best time and places to visit. Witness a French-infused city that's in love with festivals, the arts, good food, living well ...Originally called Ville-Marie, or "City of Mary", it is named after Mount Royal,the triple-peaked hill in the heart of the city. The city is centred on the Island of Montreal, which took its name from the same source as the city,and a few much smaller peripheral islands, the largest of which is Île Bizard. It has a distinct four-season continental climate with warm to hot summers and cold, snowy winters.
